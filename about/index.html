About Me.

Education

I graduated from the University of Alberta with a Bachelor of Arts (Honors)

in Economics with a Political Science minor.

Research

My research interests focus on macroeconomics, computational

economics, political economy, and data science.


I am curious about the macroeconomy and the application of

computational methods and data science to analyze and understand

economic phenomena.

Where I am now

Shortly after completing my studies at the University of Alberta,

I worked at a regional development agency called Western Economic Diversification (WD/DEO), now called Prairies Economic Development Canada (PrairiesCan), as a Junior Economist.


After working at WD/PrairiesCan for a year, I joined the Bank of Canada as a Research Assistant near the end of August 2021.

Extras

I am a first-generation Filipino-Canadian. I spent my formative years in the Philippines, at the beautiful coastal city of San Fernando, La Union.


I currently reside in Edmonton, AB.
